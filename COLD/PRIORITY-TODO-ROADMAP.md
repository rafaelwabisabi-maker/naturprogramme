## 🚨 KILL SWITCHES (Stop If...)

1. **After 30 emails, 0 responses**
   → Revise email copy or offer

2. **After 3 workshops, 0 referrals**
   → Workshop content needs improvement

3. **After 60 days, <2 bookings**
   → Reassess target market (maybe corporate instead?)

4. **Lead magnet: <10% conversion**
   → Content is wrong, revisit offer

5. **Discovery calls: <30% close rate**
   → Pricing too high or qualification too loose
