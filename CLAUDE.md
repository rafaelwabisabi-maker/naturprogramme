## ⚠️ LEGAL CONSTRAINT — Austrian Cold Email Ban (TKG 2021 § 174)
**Cold email to Austrian businesses is ILLEGAL.** This includes schools, municipalities, NGOs, Vereine.
Austria uses strict OPT-IN for all electronic commercial communication. ECG-Liste check is mandatory but does NOT replace consent.

**The ONLY legal cold outbound in Austria = physical mail.**
- **Physical mail (Briefpost)** ✅ — GDPR Legitimate Interest (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. f)
- **Phone calls** ❌ — ALSO illegal for cold marketing (no B2B exception in AT!)
- **Contact forms** ❌ — classified as unsolicited electronic communication
- **LinkedIn inbound** ✅ — post content, wait for DMs. Don't mass-message.
- **Platform listings** ✅ — NATURSCHAUSPIEL, Klimabündnis = schools come to you
- **Events** ✅ — business card exchange = consent for follow-up
- **International email** ✅ — only to opt-out jurisdictions (US, Brazil, Mexico)
- **After opt-in** ✅ — QR code from letter → landing page → double opt-in → all channels unlocked

**Consent Factory:** Letter with QR code → landing page → double opt-in → email/phone now legal.
See `digital-struktur/MARKET-ENTRY-BATTLEPLAN.md` for full strategy.

**Grant pitch model:** Write concept + budget → approach Gemeinde → they submit as Fördernehmer → grant covers NP fees.

**Alternative channel: Elternverein** — Parent associations operate independently of school budgets, can apply for different micro-grants, and have faster decision cycles than school administration. Target Elternvereine directly (also via physical mail).

**Verein requirement:** Some youth/social grants require a Verein as executing partner. Consider registering a NaturProgramme Verein for grant eligibility.
