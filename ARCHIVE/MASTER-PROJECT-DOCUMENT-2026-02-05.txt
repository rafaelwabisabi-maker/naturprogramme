6. PRICING STRATEGY (UPDATED DECISION - FEB 5, 2026)
================================================================================

-------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT WE KNOW (Facts)
-------------------------------------------------------------------

ELISABETH'S ACTUAL RATES (What she receives):
- Halbtag: €600-800
- Ganztag: €1,200-1,500

These are NOT made-up estimates. These are real rates she's been paid.

SCHOOLS/CENTERS PAY MORE:
Centers charge schools €800-1,500+ (includes their facility, meals, margin)
So Elisabeth's rates are FAIR, not expensive.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
THE PROBLEM WITH SHOWING PRICES
-------------------------------------------------------------------

ISSUE 1: Sticker Shock Before Value
- Visitor sees €800 → "Too expensive" → Leaves immediately
- No chance to understand value
- No relationship building
- No conversation opportunity

ISSUE 2: One-Size-Fits-All Impression
- Reality: Prices vary by group size, location, timing
- Showing fixed range feels inflexible
- Discourages inquiries from those who might negotiate

ISSUE 3: Austrian Education Sector Norms
- "Auf Anfrage" is VERY common in this sector
- Educational services rarely show exact pricing
- Facilitators typically quote per inquiry

-------------------------------------------------------------------
DECISION: NO PRICES ON WEBSITE (Individuelles Angebot)
-------------------------------------------------------------------

WHAT WE SHOW INSTEAD:

Programme cards say:
"Individuelles Angebot
Preis abhängig von Gruppengröße, Standort und Ihrer spezifischen Situation."

CTA: "Anfrage stellen"

-------------------------------------------------------------------
WHY THIS IS BETTER
-------------------------------------------------------------------

✅ Every interested lead enters the funnel (no price filtering)
✅ Build relationship before price discussion
✅ Can customize offer based on their budget/situation
✅ Room to negotiate for long-term partnerships
✅ Aligns with Austrian education sector norms
✅ Protects against competitor price undercutting
✅ Allows testing different price points in conversations

-------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IT WORKS IN PRACTICE
-------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1: Visitor fills out contact form
STEP 2: Elisabeth emails back within 24-48h with:
  "Basierend auf Ihrer Anfrage (25 Schüler, Halbtag, März):
   Mein Angebot: €700 (inkl. alle Materialien, Vor- und Nachbesprechung)
   Das entspricht €28 pro Schüler*in.
   Passt das in Ihr Budget?"

STEP 3: If yes → Schedule call → Book
STEP 4: If no → "Was wäre machbar?" → Negotiate if possible

-------------------------------------------------------------------
DATA COLLECTION PLAN
-------------------------------------------------------------------

After 10-20 inquiries, analyze:
- What price do most accept?
- Did lack of pricing hurt conversion?
- Do people ask about price before inquiring?

THEN DECIDE:
- Add "Ab €600" soft pricing if it helps conversion
- Keep "auf Anfrage" if it's working fine

This is ITERATIVE, not permanent.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
FALLBACK FOR PRICE QUESTIONS
-------------------------------------------------------------------

If someone emails asking "How much?":

RESPONSE TEMPLATE:
"Danke für Ihr Interesse! 
Die Investition hängt von einigen Faktoren ab:
- Gruppengröße
- Halbtag oder Ganztag
- Standort (Anreisezeit)
- Zeitraum (Wochentag vs. Wochenende)

Richtwert: Die meisten Schulen investieren zwischen €600-900 für einen 
Halbtag mit 25 Schüler*innen.

Können Sie mir mehr über Ihre Situation erzählen? Dann kann ich Ihnen 
ein genaues Angebot machen."

This gives context without committing to exact price upfront.
