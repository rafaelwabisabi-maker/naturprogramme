## 1. AUSTRIAN MUNICIPAL GRANT PROGRAMS

### 1.1 Land OOe — Bewusstseinsbildende Klimaschutzaktionen
- **What:** Funding for awareness-building climate measures in OOe Klimabuendnis municipalities
- **Who can apply:** Municipal organizations acting on behalf of OOe Klimabuendnis Gemeinden, AND non-profit Vereine/organizations/schools cooperating with at least one OOe Klimabuendnis municipality
- **Amount:** Up to 50% of eligible gross costs, max EUR 2,000 per individual activity
- **Period:** March 1, 2026 to March 31, 2028
- **Eligible activities:** Lectures, films, moderated film screenings, theater, dialogue forums, participation processes, excursions, WORKSHOPS, exhibitions
- **URL:** https://www.land-oberoesterreich.gv.at/109139.htm
- **Application:** https://e-gov.ooe.gv.at/fs_us/start.do?event=view&wfjs_enabled=false&generalid=us009klima
- **ACTIONABLE:** Elisabeth's nature pedagogy workshops qualify as "bewusstseinsbildende Massnahmen" if framed as climate education. Partner with any Klimabuendnis Gemeinde.

### 1.2 Land OOe — NATURSCHAUSPIEL Programm
- **What:** Austria's most successful nature education platform, run by Land OOe Abt. Naturschutz + IFAU (Institut fuer Angewandte Umweltbildung, Steyr)
- **Scale:** 180 Naturvermittler, 130 tours, 40 locations across OOe
- **How to join:** Submit a "Programmidee" (program idea) via https://naturschauspiel.at/programmidee-anmeldung
- **Payment:** Entgelt fuer Schutzgebietsfuehrungen (paid per guided tour)
- **URL:** https://naturschauspiel.at/
- **ACTIONABLE:** This is THE channel for nature educators in OOe. Elisabeth should submit a program idea for youth-focused nature pedagogy tours. This gives instant credibility + bookings + payment.

### 1.3 Land OOe — Jugendfoerderung (Ausserschulische Jugendarbeit)
- **What:** Funding for activities and investments in extracurricular youth work
- **Who:** Youth organizations, Vereine, municipalities
- **New guideline:** Strengthening open youth work long-term
- **URL:** https://www.land-oberoesterreich.gv.at/23160.htm
- **JugendService OOe portal:** https://www.jugendservice.at/fuer-jugendarbeit/foerderungen
- **ACTIONABLE:** If Elisabeth registers a Verein or partners with an existing Jugendverein, workshops qualify for this funding.

### 1.4 Land OOe — Umweltbildung und Bildung fuer Nachhaltigkeit
- **What:** Promotion of measures in educational institutions emphasizing environmental value from childhood
- **Focus:** Accelerating transition to sustainable lifestyles
- **URL:** https://www.land-oberoesterreich.gv.at/172614.htm
- **ACTIONABLE:** Direct alignment with Elisabeth's offer. Check specific Foerderrichtlinien for eligible applicants.

### 1.5 Land OOe — NATURSCHAUSPIEL Reisekostenzuschuss
- **What:** Travel cost subsidies for schools visiting the OOe NATURSCHAUSPIEL program
- **Budget 2026:** EUR 25,000 (75% still available as of research date)
- **ACTIONABLE:** Schools that book NATURSCHAUSPIEL tours get travel costs subsidized. If Elisabeth is a NATURSCHAUSPIEL Anbieterin, schools have ZERO cost barrier.

### 1.6 GeKAP — Gemeinde-Klimawandelanpassungs-Programm
- **What:** Climate change adaptation measures at municipal level
- **URL:** https://www.land-oberoesterreich.gv.at/236810.htm
- **ACTIONABLE:** If Elisabeth's programs include climate adaptation education (e.g., "Wie der Wald sich veraendert"), municipalities can fund her workshops through GeKAP.

### 1.7 LEADER OOe (EU Rural Development)
- **What:** EU co-funded rural development. 20 LEADER regions in OOe. EUR 210M for all 83 Austrian LEADER regions (2023-2027)
- **Eligible fields:** Agriculture, nature conservation, culture, social, youth, business/tourism, EDUCATION, energy, climate protection
- **URL:** https://www.leader.at/ | https://www.land-oberoesterreich.gv.at/83422.htm
- **How it works:** Projects must align with the local LEADER region's development strategy. Apply through the LAG (Lokale Aktionsgruppe) management
- **ACTIONABLE:** For Muehlviertel region specifically. Nature pedagogy for youth = education + nature conservation + youth. Triple alignment. Contact the LAG manager.

### 1.8 KEM — Klima- und Energie-Modellregionen
- **What:** 120+ KEM regions Austria-wide. Program of Klima- und Energiefonds
- **Education component:** "Klimaschulen" — projects with students on climate awareness
- **Structure:** KEM region + minimum 3 schools per project
- **External partners explicitly welcome:** klimaaktiv Mobil, Klimabuendnis, and OTHER external project partners
- **URL:** https://www.klimaundenergiemodellregionen.at/
- **Klimaschulen:** https://www.klimafonds.gv.at/klimaschulen-der-kem-und-klar-regionen/
- **External offers page:** https://www.klimafonds.gv.at/klimaschulen-der-kem-und-klar-regionen/angebote/
- **ACTIONABLE:** Get listed as an external Anbieter on the Klimaschulen platform. KEM managers actively seek partners for their school projects.

### 1.9 KLAR! — Klimawandel-Anpassungsmodellregionen
- **What:** 91 regions Austria-wide for climate adaptation. Education = core component
- **OOe:** 166 schools involved at all age levels
- **URL:** https://klar-anpassungsregionen.at/
- **ACTIONABLE:** KLAR! regions have dedicated budgets for awareness-building with youth. Contact KLAR! managers in OOe regions.

### 1.10 Klimabuendnis OOe
- **What:** Network of Klimabuendnis municipalities. Provides programs, workshops, tools to member Gemeinden
- **URL:** https://oberoesterreich.klimabuendnis.at/

## 2. HOW GRANT-FUNDED PROPOSALS WORK IN AUSTRIA

### 2.1 Can a service provider initiate a grant application?
**Short answer: Not directly, but YES in practice through partnership.**

The typical flow:
1. **The Foerdernehmer (grant recipient) must be the eligible entity** — usually the Gemeinde, Verein, school, or KEM/KLAR! region
2. **The service provider writes the concept, budget, application** and approaches the eligible entity saying: "Here's a fully funded program. I wrote the Foerderantrag. You sign it, submit it, and I deliver the service."
3. **This is STANDARD PRACTICE** in Austrian grant consulting. Firms like Foerderberater.at, Bernd Kopper, Senkrechtstarter, and the Kommunales Foerderzentrum all do this.

### 2.2 The "Foerderantrag" approach — practical steps
1. **Identify the grant program** (e.g., Klimaschulen, LEADER, GeKAP)
2. **Write a project concept** that fits the Foerderrichtlinien
3. **Create a budget** where Elisabeth's workshops are a line item
4. **Approach the Gemeinde/school/KEM manager** with a turnkey package: "Hier ist ein fertiger Foerderantrag. Sie bekommen ein kostenloses Programm fuer Ihre Schule/Gemeinde. Sie muessen nur unterschreiben."
5. **The Gemeinde submits** the application as Foerdernehmer
6. **Elisabeth delivers** the workshops
7. **The Gemeinde pays** Elisabeth from the grant funds received

### 2.3 Is this common?
**YES.** This is exactly how:
- Naturparke work with schools (they write the concept, schools sign on)
- NATURSCHAUSPIEL operates (centrally organized, guides are paid from program funds)
- Klimabuendnis delivers workshops to municipalities
- LEADER projects work (often external consultants write the project, LAG/Gemeinde submits)
- The Kommunales Foerderzentrum (NÖ) operates — central grant consulting FOR municipalities
- **URL Kommunales Foerderzentrum:** https://www.dorf-stadterneuerung.at/kommunales-foerderzentrum/

## 3. MUNICIPAL PROCUREMENT (Vergaberecht) — Direktvergabe

### 3.1 Schwellenwerte (Thresholds) — BVergG-Novelle 2026
The BVergG-Novelle 2026 entered force March 1, 2026. Key thresholds:

| Contract type | Direktvergabe limit | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Liefer- und Dienstleistungen** | **EUR 140,000** (BVergG 2026 permanent) | Previously EUR 100,000, then temporarily EUR 143,000 |
| **Bauauftraege** | EUR 300,000 | |
| **Besondere Dienstleistungen** | EUR 100,000 | Unchanged |
| **Over EUR 50,000** | 3 Vergleichsangebote required | New documentation requirement |

**What this means for Elisabeth/Rafael:**
- A Buergermeister CAN directly hire a workshop provider for up to EUR 140,000 without public tender
- Below EUR 50,000: basically a handshake deal (Direktvergabe, minimal documentation)
- EUR 50,000-140,000: still Direktvergabe, but must document 3 comparison offers/quotes
- Elisabeth's workshops (EUR 600-1,500 each) are FAR below any threshold. A Gemeinde can book her with ZERO procurement process.

### 3.2 Practical implications
- **Single workshops (EUR 600-1,500):** Buergermeister signs, done. No process needed.
- **Annual program (e.g., 10 workshops = EUR 6,000-15,000):** Still Direktvergabe, no issues.
- **Large project (EUR 20,000-50,000):** Direktvergabe, minimal documentation.
- **Digital Struktur projects (EUR 2,500-25,000):** All within Direktvergabe. Gemeinde can hire directly.

### 3.3 Typical Gemeinde budgets (estimated from knowledge)
- **Small Gemeinde (1,000-3,000 EW):** Total budget EUR 3-8M. Jugendarbeit/Bildung: EUR 10,000-50,000/year
- **Medium Gemeinde (3,000-10,000 EW):** Total budget EUR 8-25M. Jugendarbeit/Bildung: EUR 30,000-150,000/year
- **Stadt (10,000+):** EUR 25M+. Dedicated Jugendreferat with EUR 100,000-500,000+
- **Umwelt/Klima budgets:** Typically EUR 5,000-30,000 for awareness/workshops in small Gemeinden, more in Klimabuendnis-Gemeinden with dedicated KEM/KLAR! budgets

### 4.3 Legal Outreach Methods (TKG 2021 compliant)
Per naturprogramme/CLAUDE.md — cold email to Austrian entities is ILLEGAL (TKG 2021 Paragraph 174).

**Legal channels:**
1. **Physical mail (Briefpost)** — GDPR Legitimate Interest (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. f). BEST channel for first contact.
2. **Phone calls (B2B)** — Legal in Austria. Call Buergermeister/Amtsleiter directly.
3. **Events** — Gemeindetag, Kommunalmesse, WKO events, PH Fortbildungen
4. **Platform listings** — NATURSCHAUSPIEL, Klimaschulen external offers, Klimabuendnis catalog
5. **Referrals** — Through Naturparke, existing Klimabuendnis relationships, PH contacts
6. **LinkedIn** — Gray area but tolerated for professional outreach

## 5. THE "FOERDERANTRAG" APPROACH — Tactical Playbook

### 5.1 The pitch to a Gemeinde
**Script (phone or physical mail):**

> "Guten Tag, mein Name ist Elisabeth Braunschmid, ich bin diplomierte Sozialarbeiterin und zertifizierte Wildnispaedagogin. Ich biete Naturpaedagogik-Workshops fuer Jugendliche an.
>
> Ich habe gesehen, dass [Gemeindename] Klimabuendnis-Gemeinde ist. Es gibt aktuell eine Foerderung vom Land OOe fuer bewusstseinsbildende Klimamassnahmen — bis zu 50% der Kosten, max. EUR 2.000 pro Aktivitaet.
>
> Ich koennte fuer Sie einen Antrag vorbereiten. Fuer die Gemeinde waere der Workshop dann halb so teuer oder sogar kostenlos, je nach Foerderzusage. Haetten Sie Interesse?"

### 5.2 The pitch to a KEM/KLAR! manager
> "Ich bin Naturpaedagogin und biete Workshops zu Klimawandelanpassung fuer Schulen an. Ich weiss, dass KEM-Regionen Klimaschulen-Projekte umsetzen. Koennte ich als externe Partnerin in Ihr naechstes Klimaschulen-Projekt eingebunden werden?"

### 5.3 The pitch to a school (via Bildungsdirektion or PH)
> "Ich biete lehrplankonforme Naturpaedagogik-Workshops an. Ueber das NATURSCHAUSPIEL-Programm koennen Reisekosten gefoerdert werden, und ueber Klimaschulen kann das gesamte Projekt finanziert werden. Fuer die Schule entstehen keine Kosten."

### 5.4 How other nature education providers reach schools in OOe
1. **NATURSCHAUSPIEL** — THE platform. 130 tours, schools book through the website. Travel costs subsidized.
2. **Klimabuendnis** — Offers catalog to member Gemeinden/schools
3. **Naturparke** — Naturparkschule concept: permanent cooperation agreement between Naturpark and school. External partners (farmers, craftspeople, educators) are subcontracted.
4. **Nationalparks** — Dedicated education programs (Nationalpark Kalkalpen, OOe)
5. **FORUM Umweltbildung** — National platform for environmental education (BMBWF)
6. **OeKOLOG** — School network for sustainability education (schools self-select programs)
7. **Klimafonds Klimaschulen** — KEM manager coordinates, hires external partners

### 5.5 Is there a central booking platform for school programs in OOe?
- **NATURSCHAUSPIEL** (https://naturschauspiel.at/) is the closest thing — centralized booking for nature education tours
- **Bildungsdirektion OOe** (https://www.bildung-ooe.gv.at/) publishes Infomails about approved programs
- **PH OOe Fortbildungskatalog** — teachers browse and book Fortbildungen, some from external providers
- **No single "Schulprogramme marketplace"** exists in OOe. Distribution is fragmented across the channels above.
