## The Big Idea

**NaturProgramme is structured wilderness pedagogy — a nature-based education program that turns forests, rivers, and mountains into classrooms.**

This is the *least speculative* project in the portfolio. The work is already happening. Ellie leads it. Children attend. Results exist. The grant opportunity is to *fund what already exists* and document it as scalable methodology.

The insight: Europe has an enormous funded appetite for "nature-based education" and "outdoor learning" — and almost no quality providers who can speak both the pedagogical language AND the EU grant language simultaneously. NaturProgramme can do both.

---

## The Program

**Who:** Children 4-14, school groups, family weekend programs
**Where:** Mühlviertel forests, Upper Austrian wilderness areas
**What:** Structured half-day and full-day nature immersion programs

### Core Program Modules

**Waldsinne (Forest Senses)**
Guided sensory exploration. Children use all senses to experience forest — smell of decomposing leaves, sound of water, texture of bark. Curriculum connects to Austrian biology and science standards.

**Wildtier-Detektive (Wildlife Detectives)**
Tracking, observation, habitat identification. Children document findings in illustrated nature journals. Cross-curricular: science + art + writing.

**Feuer & Wasser (Fire & Water)**
Fire safety, primitive fire-making, water sourcing and purification basics. Practical wilderness skills framed as cultural heritage (how Austrians historically lived in these landscapes).

**Jahreszeiten-Wandel (Seasons of Change)**
Year-round program: each season explores different ecological phenomena. Forces program continuity — schools book for the full year.

## Grant Strategy

**EU ERASMUS+ (Education)**
- Nature-based education explicitly listed as innovative learning methodology
- Cross-border partnerships (AT + DE + CH = automatic bonus)
- Budget: up to €300K for innovation projects

**BM Bildung (Austrian Federal Ministry of Education)**
- Annual call for "innovative teaching programs"
- NaturProgramme = outdoor extension of official curriculum
- Target: accreditation as supplementary school program

**Land Oberösterreich — Landesbaudirektion**
- Regional development funding for cultural/educational programs
- Local credibility = key advantage over outside applicants

**LEADER (Rural Development)**
- Educational tourism: NaturProgramme brings families to rural Mühlviertel
- Economic impact on local accommodation, food, services
- Gemeinde co-funding possible (same strategy as DorfPlatz)

**Dual-applicant strategy:**
- **Ellie** applies as pedagogical lead (cultural association)
- **Rafael** applies as digital infrastructure partner (tech co-applicant)
- Two separate bites at every grant call
